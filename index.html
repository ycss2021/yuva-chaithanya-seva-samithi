About YCSS

Yuva Chaithanya Seva Samithi (YCSS) is a registered non-profit organization established in 2021 under the Andhra Pradesh Societies Registration Act. We work in the areas of Child Rights, Health, Education, Women Empowerment, Livelihood Promotion, Tribal Development and Community Development.

Our Vision

To build an inclusive, equitable and sustainable society where every individual enjoys dignity, equality and opportunity.

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable communities through sustainable interventions in Health, Education, Child Protection, Women Empowerment, Livelihood and Environment.

Community Health and Nutrition.

Education Support and Digital Literacy.

Protection and Child Safety.

👩 Women Empowerment

Skill Development and SHG Support.

Natural Farming and Entrepreneurship.
