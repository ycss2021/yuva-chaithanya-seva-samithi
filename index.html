About YCSS

Yuva Chaithanya Seva Samithi (YCSS) is a registered non-profit, non-political and community-based organization established in 2021 under the Andhra Pradesh Societies Registration Act, 2001. YCSS works at the grassroots level to improve the lives of women, children, youth, tribal communities and economically disadvantaged families through sustainable development programmes. Our key focus areas include Child Rights & Protection, Health & Nutrition, Education, Women Empowerment, Livelihood Promotion, Environmental Conservation and Community Development. We work in partnership with Government Departments, CSR Foundations, educational institutions and local communities to build an inclusive, healthy and self-reliant society.

Our Vision

To build an inclusive, equitable and sustainable society where every individual, especially women, children, youth and marginalized communities, enjoys equal opportunities, dignity, protection and a better quality of life.

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable communities through sustainable interventions in Health, Education, Child Protection, Women Empowerment, Livelihood Promotion, Environmental Conservation and Community Participation, enabling them to become self-reliant and contribute to inclusive development.

Our Core Program Areas

🏥 Health & Nutrition

Health awareness, medical camps, nutrition education, HIV/AIDS awareness and community health programmes.

📚 Education

School support, digital literacy, scholarships, career guidance and quality education initiatives.

👧 Child Rights & Protection

Child protection, child safety awareness, prevention of child labour and child marriage.

👩 Women Empowerment

Skill development, SHG strengthening, financial literacy and women leadership programmes.

🌱 Livelihood Promotion

Entrepreneurship development, natural farming, skill training and income generation activities.

🌍 Environment

Tree plantation, climate awareness, water conservation and sustainable development initiatives.
