About YCSS

Yuva Chaithanya Seva Samithi (YCSS) is a registered non-profit organization established in 2021 under the Andhra Pradesh Societies Registration Act. We work in Child Rights, Health, Education, Women Empowerment, Livelihood Promotion, Tribal Development and Community Development across Andhra Pradesh.

Our Vision

To build an inclusive, equitable and sustainable society where every individual enjoys dignity, equality and opportunity.

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable communities through sustainable interventions in Health, Education, Child Protection, Women Empowerment, Livelihood and Environment.

Our Core Programme Areas

🏥 Health

Community Health, Nutrition and Awareness.

📚 Education

School Education, Scholarships & Digital Literacy.

👧 Child Rights

Child Protection & Safety Awareness.

👩 Women Empowerment

Skill Development & SHG Strengthening.
