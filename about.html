About YCSS

Yuva Chaithanya Seva Samithi (YCSS) is a registered non-profit organization established in 2021 under the Andhra Pradesh Societies Registration Act (Registration No. 322/2021). The organization works with a vision of building an inclusive, equitable and sustainable society where every individual can live with dignity, opportunity and security.

YCSS works closely with children, women, youth, tribal communities, schools, self-help groups and vulnerable families by implementing community-based development programmes across Andhra Pradesh.

Our Vision

To create a healthy, educated, empowered and self-reliant society by promoting equality, justice, child protection and sustainable development.

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable communities through quality interventions in health, education, women empowerment, livelihood promotion, environmental conservation and child protection.

Our Core Values

Integrity

Transparency and accountability in every activity.

Respect

Equal respect for every individual without discrimination.

Innovation

Creative solutions for community development.

Participation

Working together with communities and stakeholders.

Sustainability

Long-term development through local ownership.

Child First

Ensuring every child's rights, safety and development.
