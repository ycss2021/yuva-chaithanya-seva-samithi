About YCSS

Yuva Chaithanya Seva Samithi (YCSS) is a registered non-profit, non-political and community-based organization established in 2021 under the Andhra Pradesh Societies Registration Act, 2001.

YCSS works at the grassroots level to improve the lives of women, children, youth, tribal communities and economically disadvantaged families through sustainable development programmes.

Our key focus areas include:

  • Child Rights & Protection
  • Health & Nutrition
  • Quality Education
  • Women Empowerment
  • Youth Development
  • Livelihood Promotion
  • Natural Farming
  • Environmental Conservation
  • Community Development

Our Vision

To build an inclusive, equitable and sustainable society where every individual enjoys equal opportunities, dignity and protection.

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable communities through sustainable interventions in Health, Education, Child Protection, Women Empowerment, Livelihood, Environment and Community Participation.
