Our Commitment to Transparency

YCSS follows the principles of transparency, accountability and responsible governance. We regularly document programme activities, maintain financial records and comply with statutory requirements. Reports and publications will be updated periodically to ensure that donors, CSR partners, government agencies and the public have access to accurate information.
