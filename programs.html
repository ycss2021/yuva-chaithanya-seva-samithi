Our Programmes

YCSS implements community-based development programmes focusing on improving the quality of life of children, women, youth, tribal communities and vulnerable families.

🏥 Health & Nutrition

Medical camps, health awareness, HIV/AIDS awareness, adolescent health, nutrition and preventive healthcare programmes.

📚 Education

School education support, scholarships, digital literacy, career guidance and learning enhancement.

👧 Child Rights & Protection

Child rights awareness, POCSO awareness, prevention of child labour, child marriage and child abuse.

👩 Women Empowerment

Skill development, SHG strengthening, financial literacy, leadership development and entrepreneurship.

🌱 Livelihood Promotion

Natural farming, MEDP, LEDP, entrepreneurship development and sustainable income generation.

🌍 Environment

Tree plantation, climate awareness, biodiversity conservation and environmental sustainability.

💻 Digital Safety

Cyber safety, responsible internet usage, online child protection and digital awareness.

🏫 SHE Programme

School Health Education Programme focusing on adolescent health, menstrual hygiene and life skills.

🤝 Tribal Development

Integrated development initiatives for tribal communities through education, health and livelihood.
