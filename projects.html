Our Flagship Projects

YCSS implements innovative and sustainable community development projects focusing on child protection, education, health, livelihood, women empowerment and tribal development.

🛡️ Surakshita Bala

A child protection initiative promoting child rights, POCSO awareness, prevention of child labour, child marriage and child abuse.

🏫 School Health Education (SHE)

Improving adolescent health awareness, menstrual hygiene, nutrition education and life skills among school children.

💻 SafeTech – Digital Safety

Creating awareness on cyber safety, responsible internet usage and online child protection.

🌸 Floral Harmony (MEDP)

Skill development programme on bouquet making, floral decoration and flower preservation supported under MEDP initiatives.

🍌 Banana Fibre Enterprise

Promoting banana pseudo-stem fibre extraction and value addition to create sustainable livelihoods for rural communities.

🏥 Community Health Programme

Health camps, nutrition awareness, preventive healthcare and community health education programmes.

🌿 Tribal Development

Integrated development interventions for tribal communities focusing on education, health and livelihood promotion.

🤝 Women Empowerment

Strengthening women through SHGs, financial literacy, entrepreneurship and leadership development.

🌍 Environmental Conservation

Tree plantation, climate awareness, water conservation and environmental sustainability activities.
